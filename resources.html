RESOURCES

Read about queer Asian fashion creators, brands, and Asian LGBTQ+ organizations!






Alok Menon is an Indian fashionista known for their experimental style, vibrant colours, South Asian patterns and daring fits.

Maitrayanee Mahanta is a ravishing Maitrayanee creator who is an avid advocator of LGBTQ+ inclusivity.

Koen Danai are a Thai LGBTQ+ Activist and training Psychotherapists, known for their flamboyant, colorful style as well as endless creativity.

Bretman Rock is a social media influencer known for his humorous, unapologetic personality and his iconic style.

Chella Man is a deaf, trans, Chinese and Jewish individual, Man has relentlessly advocated for inclusivity through art.

Nara Kim is a stylist, fashion influencer, queer activist and artist who lives in South Korea.





SYRO is a badass femme footwear brand fighting Asian stereotypes and fetishization in white culture that deem Asians to be “exotic”

EDWIN is one of the first fashion brands that celebrate LGBTQ, Edwin was established in Japan in the 1940s and manufactured the first denim jeans inclusive for all.

AJOBYAJO is one of the few — if not only — fashion labels in Korea that openly promotes LGBTQ+ identity, founded by Ajo Kim in 2016.






The AAPI - It Gets Better Project is a nonprofit organization with a mission to uplift, empower, and connect LGBTQ+ youthS around the globe.
